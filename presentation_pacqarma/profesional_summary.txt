Dr. Shibam Ghosh is a Postdoctoral Researcher at INRIA Paris, working in the COSMIQ team with Léo Perrin.
He recently completed his PhD at the University of Haifa under the supervision of Prof. Orr Dunkelman, where his thesis focused on mathematical techniques in symmetric-key cryptanalysis.
His doctoral work includes security analyses of modern standards such as AES, SHA-3 along with desing and analysis of various lightweight block ciphers including NIST LwC candidates.
Before starting his PhD, Dr. Ghosh earned an M.Tech in Cryptology and Security from ISI Kolkata and an M.Sc. in Mathematics from Presidency University.
His research in symmetric-key cryptography spans design and analysis of low-latency primitives, implementation attacks, automated cryptanalysis, and design and analysis of zero-knowledge–friendly primitives.
He has co-designed low-latency block ciphers such as QARMAv2 and ChiLow, and his work has appeared at leading venues including EUROCRYPT, FSE, CHES as well as in ACNS, Latincrypt and Indocrypt.
Dr. Ghosh is also active in the community as a reviewer and program committee member for venues like LightSEC, International Security Conference, DCC and FFA.
